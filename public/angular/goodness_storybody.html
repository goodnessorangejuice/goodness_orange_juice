Our Story

Goodness Fresh Orange Juice was conceived out of passion and need to provide a healthy affordable drink for both kids and adults. With the global health statistics showing an increasing rate of obesity and diabetes, plus the limited healthy drink options in the market, Goodness Fresh Orange Juice is definitely the way to go!

Co-founders Kizito & Lilian who are fitness enthusiasts and had been juicing for many years, believe that taking care of the body begins first from the inside which includes essentially, being conscious of what we eat and drink. With Goodness Fresh Orange Juice, we are feeding our nation with pure natural goodness and what’s more, at an amazing affordable price of N200 per bottle (500ml).

Our Mission is simple:

Make it Natural, Affordable and for Everyone!

We are proud to be the number one national 100% cold – pressed orange juice brand. Every single orange is grown, picked and squeezed right here in Nigeria. You can taste the difference in every sip. Learn more about what we do, right Here.
